Privacy Policy for the Anime Discord Bot

Last updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Anime Discord Bot (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects user information. By using the Bot, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect limited information required for functionality, such as:

  • Discord user IDs and usernames
  • Server IDs where the Bot is added
  • Command usage data (e.g., how often a command is used)

The Bot does not collect personal data like email addresses, passwords, or payment information.

3. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used only to:

  • Provide and improve Bot functionality
  • Prevent abuse or misuse of commands
  • Monitor stability and performance

Data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Temporary logs may be stored for debugging or maintenance purposes and are automatically deleted after a short period. No permanent databases are used for user tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

The Bot may use public APIs (such as MyAnimeList or HiAnime) to fetch anime information. These services have their own privacy policies, and the Bot does not control their data practices.

6. User Control

Users may remove the Bot from their server at any time, which immediately stops all data collection for that server. No user-specific data remains stored after removal.

7. Children’s Privacy

The Bot is intended for general audiences but should not be used by individuals under 13 years of age without parental permission, per Discord’s Terms of Service.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Bot after changes are made signifies your acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact the developer through Discord or via the official support channel for the Bot.
